Medberry Privacy Policy

Last updated: 01 Sep 2025

1. Who is responsible for your data

This Privacy Policy applies to the personal data that Medberry collects and uses. References in this Privacy Policy to “we”, “us” or “our” mean Medberry. Medberry is a limited company registered in the UK at Companies House (registration number 09382108). We control the ways that your personal data are collected and the purposes for which they are used, and are the “data controller” for the purposes of the UK Data Protection Act 1998, the General Data Protection Regulation 2018 and other applicable European data protection legislation.

We are registered as a data controller with the UK Information Commissioner’s Office. Our data protection registration number is TBC.

2. Personal data we collect about you

“Personal data” means information that relates to you and allows us to identify you, either directly or in combination with other information. Your personal data may include your name, email, hospital location, grade, or your Twitter or Facebook ID if you registered via those routes, as well as information on how you use our website and app or interact with us.

Categories of data we collect

  • Email address, Facebook or Twitter ID if you registered using social logins.
  • Your name, location, and grade when producing a report.
  • Your feedback when you respond to requests.
  • Website usage information via cookies.

3. Facebook Login data

If you choose to log in via Facebook, we may receive your name, email address, Facebook user ID, and profile picture (if granted). We use this information solely to authenticate you, create/manage your Medberry account, and provide access to the app. This data is not shared outside Medberry except as described in this Privacy Policy.

4. How and why we use your personal data

  • Provide secure access: we use login details to secure your account.
  • Communicate with you: we may email you about important updates or service changes.
  • Provide support: we use your email to provide support, including via Zendesk.com (our support provider).
  • Inform you of updates: occasional information about improvements or new products.
  • Legal obligations & operations: safety, security, statistical analysis, system testing, maintenance and development.

5. Your rights and data deletion

You have a right to access and delete the personal data we hold about you. This process is free of charge unless the request is manifestly unfounded or excessive. For how to delete your data (including data received via Facebook Login), see our Data Deletion Instructions. For further information, email support@medberry.co.uk.

6. Security of your personal data

We take appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised processing, accidental loss, destruction, or damage. Data is transmitted securely using encryption. Data may be accessed by approved third parties (e.g. hosting providers) and may be transferred outside the EEA with safeguards in place.

7. Cookies and tracking technologies

We use cookies, pixels and analytics to improve our services and understand how users interact with our site, app, and emails. You can delete cookies via your browser settings; most features will remain accessible without them. For more information, see our Cookie Policy.

8. Sharing your personal data

We only share your data where necessary to provide our service:

  • Apple (subscriptions): see their Privacy Policy. We may share payment data with our payment services provider as needed to process payments, refunds, and related queries.
  • Zendesk (support): we use their platform for ticketing; you may register to track tickets. See Zendesk’s privacy statement for how they handle your data.
  • Research partners: we may work with partners to analyse Medberry datasets as outlined in our terms; we do not share identifiable personal data.

9. International transfers and retention

Your personal data may be accessed and processed outside the EEA (including by staff operating outside the EEA who work for us). Where data are transferred outside of the EEA/UK, appropriate safeguards (e.g., Standard Contractual Clauses) are used. We retain personal data as long as necessary for the purposes set out in this Policy or to comply with law.

10. Updates to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will publish any new versions on this page and update the “Last updated” date.

11. Auto-renewal subscriptions

Medberry services may use auto-renewable subscriptions. Users can manage or cancel subscriptions by following Apple’s instructions: Apple Subscription Support.

12. Contact information

Questions, comments, or requests regarding this policy are welcome: support@medberry.co.uk.

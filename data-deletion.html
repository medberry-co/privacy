Facebook Data Deletion — Medberry

Option A (in-app): Open Medberry → Settings → Delete Account and Data Request. This permanently deletes your account and associated data, including data received via Facebook Login.

Option B (email): Email support@medberry.co.uk from your account email with subject “Facebook Data Deletion – Medberry.” We’ll verify your request and delete your data within 30 days.

You may also remove Medberry access in your Facebook settings (Settings > Apps and websites).
